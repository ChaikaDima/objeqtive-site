Objeqtive is a product photography studio based in Warsaw. We specialize in creating high-quality catalog and advertising images that enhance brand identity and influence customer purchasing decisions.

We combine technical precision with aesthetics, working with both small brands and larger companies that want to ensure a consistent, professional presentation of their products — both online and offline.

In our studio, we create photographs for online stores and catalogs, advertising campaigns and social media, as well as product presentations on websites, in print, and PR materials. Every image is the result of a thoughtful process — light, form, detail, and mood. We take care of every stage, from product preparation and styling to the final post-production.

Objeqtive is more than just photography — it`s a way of visual thinking. It`s minimalism, light, and a focus on what truly matters. From clean, straightforward product shots to complex, creative productions — we adapt our approach to suit your needs and ensure your product is presented at its very best.
